        subject who performed the activity for each window sample. Its range is from 1 to 30. 

"activity.label"
        Activity labels range from 1:6

"activity.description"
        Names of activity:
        1 WALKING
        2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS
        3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
        4 SITTING
        5 STANDING
        6 LAYING
